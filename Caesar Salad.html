Caesar Salad


Ingredients – Salad :

Ingredient Amount
Romaine lettuce (washed and chopped) 1 large head
Croutons (toasted bread cubes) 1 cup
Grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup

Ingredients – Dressing :

Ingredient : Amount
Mayonnaise 1/2 cup
Lemon juice 2 tablespoons
Dijon mustard 1 teaspoon
Garlic (minced) 1–2 cloves
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Salt and black pepper To taste
Anchovy fillets (optional, mashed) 2

Recipe :

  1. Make the dressing: In a bowl, mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. If using anchovies, mash and add them too. Mix well until creamy.
  2. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, add the chopped lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Add the dressing: Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently until everything is coated.
  4. Serve: Serve immediately, optionally topped with grilled chicken or more cheese.
