Mushroom Soup


Ingredients :

Ingredient Amount
Fresh mushrooms (sliced)250 grams
Small onion (finely chopped)1
Butter or oil2 tablespoons
All-purpose flour2 tablespoons
Milk2 cups
Water or broth1 cup
Cooking cream (optional)1/2 cup
Salt and black pepperTo taste
Nutmeg (optional)A pinch

Recipe :

  1. Sauté the vegetables: Melt butter in a pot and cook chopped onion until soft. Add mushrooms and sauté 5–7 minutes.
  2. Add flour: Sprinkle flour over mushrooms, stir for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add liquids: Gradually pour in milk and broth while stirring to avoid lumps. Simmer 10–15 minutes.
  4. Blend (optional): Blend the soup for a smooth texture, or leave it chunky.
  5. Add cream (optional): Stir in cream and nutmeg. Season with salt and pepper. Heat gently.
  6. Serve: Serve hot, optionally with bread or croutons.
