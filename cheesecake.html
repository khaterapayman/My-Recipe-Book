Cheese Cake

Ingredients :

Ingredient Amount
Plain Biscuits 200g
Butter 100g
Cream Cheese 300g
Sugar 1/2 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla 1 teaspoon

Recipe :

  1. Crush the biscuits and mix with melted butter.
  2. Press the mixture into the bottom of a shallow cake pan and refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  3. Mix cream cheese with sugar and vanilla, then add eggs.
  4. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the biscuits and bake in a preheated oven at 170°C (340°F) for 40 minutes.
  5. After cooling, refrigerate the cheesecake and slice before serving.
