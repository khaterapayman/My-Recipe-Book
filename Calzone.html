Calzone


Ingredients – Dough :

Ingredient Amount
Active dry yeast 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet)
Warm water (about 43°C) 1 cup
All-purpose flour 2 ½ cups
Sugar 1 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Olive oil 1 tablespoon

Ingredients – Filling (adjust as you like) :

Ingredient Amount
Shredded mozzarella cheese 1 cup
Ricotta cheese ½ cup
Cooked vegetables (mushrooms, spinach, etc.) ½ cup
Cooked chicken, sausage, or pepperoni (optional) ½ cup
Dried oregano or Italian seasoning ½ teaspoon
Salt and pepper To taste
Tomato sauce (for dipping or inside) As needed

Recipe :

  1. Make the Dough: In a bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy. Add the flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead for 5–7 minutes until smooth. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Filling: Mix mozzarella, ricotta, vegetables, meat (if using), and seasonings.
  3. Shape the Calzones: Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Divide dough into 2–4 parts and roll each into a circle. Add filling on one half, fold, and seal edges. Crimp with a fork.
  4. Bake: Place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. (Optional) Brush tops with olive oil or egg wash. Bake 15–20 minutes until golden brown.
  5. Serve: Let cool slightly and serve warm with tomato sauce.
