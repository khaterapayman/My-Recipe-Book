Beef Pizza

Ingredients :

Ingredient Amount
Pizza dough Ready-made or homemade
Ground beef 250g
Onion (finely chopped) 1 medium
Bell pepper 1
Mushrooms 100g
Pizza cheese As needed
Tomato or pizza sauce As needed
Salt, pepper, turmeric, oregano To taste
Oil (for sautéing) A little

Recipe:

  1. Sauté the chopped onion in a little oil until soft. Then add the ground beef and spices and cook until done.
  2. Separately sauté the bell pepper and mushrooms lightly.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough on a tray and spread pizza sauce on top.
  4. Add the cooked meat, bell pepper, and mushrooms over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle pizza cheese generously on top.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for about 20 minutes, until the cheese is melted and golden.
