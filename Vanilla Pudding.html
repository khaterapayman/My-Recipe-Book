Vanilla Pudding

ingredients :

Ingredients Amount
Milk 2 Cups
Suger 1/2 Cup
Cornstarch 2 tablespoons
Vanilla 1 Teaspoon
Eggs 2

Recipe :

  1. In a saucepan, combine milk and heat until warm.
  2. Mix cornstarch with a little cold milk to prevent lumps.
  3. Beat eggs with suger and add to the warm milk mixture.
  4. Stir the mixture over medium heat until the pudding thickens.
  5. Finally, add vanilla and pour the pudding into serving cups. Let it cool.
